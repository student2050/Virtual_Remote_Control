🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

Summary: TV Remote does NOT collect, store, or share any personal data. The app works 100% locally on your WiFi network.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. TV Remote operates entirely on your local WiFi network and does not transmit any data to external servers.

2. How the App Works

TV Remote connects directly from your device to your Samsung Smart TV via your local WiFi network using the Samsung WebSocket API. The connection is:

  • Direct (device to TV, no intermediary servers)
  • Local (within your home network only)
  • Encrypted (using WSS/TLS when supported by the TV)

3. Data Storage

The only data stored is saved locally on your device:

  • TV IP Address: Stored in your device's local storage for reconnection convenience
  • TV Port: Stored locally for connection settings
  • TV MAC Address: Optionally stored for Wake-on-LAN functionality
  • Connection Token: Stored locally for automatic reconnection to your TV

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app.

4. Network Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

  • Internet Access: Required to establish WebSocket connections to your TV on the local network
  • WiFi State: Used to detect your local network for TV discovery
  • Network State: Used to monitor connection status

5. Third-Party Services

TV Remote does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no ads in the app.

6. Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any data from any user, including children. There is no account creation or personal information required.

7. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data in the first place.

8. Security

All communications between your device and TV use the Samsung WebSocket protocol. When connecting via port 8002, the connection is encrypted using WSS (WebSocket Secure).

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
